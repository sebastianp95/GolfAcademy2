What we teach

In all sports, values such as respect, fair play, friendship, and discipline need to be present. We like coaching tips, such as dieting, managing attitudes, strategies, and behaviors on the field. Dominate each area of the game, Short Game (chip, pitch, bunker), Long game (driver) and Putt.

I am William Buitrago

A Golf Instructor with 25 years of teaching experience. I went through all stages of the Golf career, caddy, aspiring player, professional player, and then instructor; Since then, I have trained year after year, adding knowledge and experience. I obtained my TPI certification level one workshop and level two junior professional workshop. I worked at the High-Performance Center in the Colombian Golf Federation, where I started as an instructor to become the director of the infant and young adult schools

Our Goal

We are trying to provide you with all the necessary tools to raise your game while keeping a strong foundation. With your current swing, we analyze and correct by repeating exercises and videos of each student, explaining their strengths and technical aspects to be improved.

Start today

Our school offers a highly regulated instructional program, based on a personalized work plan. Using an individualized approach, each student's program is personalized and provides a follow-up for our coaches to guide, facilitate and supervise. We believe that a positive learning experience is the result of open and ongoing communication, regular review of work, and a partnership between students, parents, and our coaches.
